Contract

The agency agreement — what Alohi has signed, what she owes them, what they owe her, and what it takes to get out if needed.

Agency call — date TBD The goal of that call is to fully understand the contract before making any major decisions — especially before July 17th. Drop the contract document and call notes here afterward.

Questions to Get Answered on the Call

These are the non-negotiables. Every answer should be documented in the Calls folder.

  • What is the exact commission rate? On which types of income?
  • Does commission apply to creator-sourced deals or only agency-sourced?
  • Does commission apply to TikTok/Instagram direct monetization?
  • Does commission apply to merch sales?
  • What is the contract term length? When does it expire?
  • What is the exit clause? How many days notice? Any penalties?
  • Who owns the content IP — Alohi or the agency?
  • Is there an exclusivity clause? What does it cover?
  • What exactly is the 65-hour streaming requirement? Is it enforceable?
  • What happens if she doesn't hit 65 hours in a given month?
  • What is the agency actively doing to generate deal flow right now?

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Commission on ALL revenue regardless of who sourced the deal
  • Long exclusivity clauses with no carve-outs
  • Agency claims ownership of any content or brand identity
  • No clear exit clause or exit requires agency approval
  • Vague performance expectations with no accountability on their side
  • Automatic renewal language buried in the agreement

Acceptable Terms (Reference)

  • Commission: 20% maximum on agency-sourced deals only
  • 0–10% on creator-sourced deals
  • Exit clause: 30–60 days notice, no financial penalty
  • IP: Alohi retains full ownership of all content and brand identity
  • No exclusivity on merch, direct fan income, or personal ventures
