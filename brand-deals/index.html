Brand Deals

Sponsorship pipeline — from first contact to closed deal. The goal is to treat this like sales, not luck. Consistent outreach, confident pricing, and repeat business.

The biggest opportunity right now Alohi has 1M+ TikTok followers and is under-monetized. Brand deals are the fastest path to real income. The key is being proactive — most creators wait for inbound. The ones who win send 25–50 pitches a week.

Rate Card

Starting rates — state these confidently and never apologize for them. Never immediately discount; reduce scope instead.

  • Single TikTok post: $5,000
  • TikTok + Instagram bundle: $6,500–$8,000
  • Usage rights (brand can repurpose content): +30–100%
  • Exclusivity (can't work with competitors): +20–50%
  • TikTok LIVE integration: negotiate separately

Target Brand Categories

Brands most aligned with Alohi's audience and content niche.

  • Anime merchandise and figure manufacturers
  • Gaming companies and streaming platforms (Twitch, etc.)
  • Cosplay and costume suppliers
  • Apparel and streetwear brands
  • Convention organizers (Anime Expo, Fan Expo, etc.)
  • Collectibles and limited edition releases
  • Beauty and makeup brands
  • Tech accessories (LED, ring lights, stream gear)

Pipeline Stages

  • Prospecting — brands identified, not yet contacted
  • Outreach sent — pitch email or DM sent, awaiting response
  • In conversation — brand responded, discussing scope/budget
  • Negotiating — offer on the table, counter in progress
  • Closed / Won — deal signed, invoice sent
  • Delivered — content posted, payment received
  • Follow-up — post-campaign check-in for repeat business

Inbound Inquiry Response

Every brand inquiry gets this response first — it filters low-budget leads and establishes professionalism before any pricing is shared:

  • Thanks for reaching out — what are the campaign goals?
  • What deliverables are you looking for?
  • What's the timeline?
  • What's the budget range?
  • Are usage rights or exclusivity required?
