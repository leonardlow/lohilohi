Call Log Format

Each call note should follow this structure for consistency:

  • Date — YYYY-MM-DD
  • Participants — who was on the call
  • Purpose — why the call happened
  • Key points — what was discussed, verbatim where possible
  • Decisions made — anything agreed or committed to
  • Action items — who does what by when
  • Follow-up needed — open questions or unresolved items
