Agency Fact-Finding Questions

For use during the call with Wawa Productions. This is a fact-finding mission — not a negotiation. Tone should remain professional and neutral throughout. Take notes in the answer fields as they respond.

Before the call: Review Alohi's answers first so you know where the gaps are going in.
After the call: Send a written email summarizing everything discussed. That email is your paper trail.

The Contract & Term

Establish the facts of the current agreement before anything else.

"Can you confirm the current contract runs from April 1, 2026 through April 1, 2027?"

Listen for: any hesitation, a different date range, or mention of amendments.

Do not reveal that she's attended conventions without clearing them. Let them describe their process first. If they describe a process she hasn't been following, that's useful information.
