To: Warens — Wawa Productions
From: Alohi Barboza — lohilohisan@gmail.com
Subject: Follow-up from our meeting — a few questions
Hi Warens, It was really great meeting with my parents on Saturday — thank you for taking the time. I appreciate you being open to having that conversation with us. After the meeting I wanted to follow up with a few questions in writing, just so I have a clear record of how things work as I get more organized on the business side. None of this is urgent, but I'd love to have clear answers so I can plan properly. 1. Commission structure Can you walk me through exactly how the commission breakdown works? I want to make sure I understand who receives what percentage on each deal, and what the 1.5% administrative fee covers. I'm trying to get better at budgeting and forecasting my income, so having this clearly documented would really help. 2. TikTok LIVE and platform income Just to confirm in writing — income I earn directly through TikTok LIVE (fan gifts, subscriptions, and milestone payouts from campaigns like the Creator Career Program) is fully mine and not subject to agency commission, correct? I want to make sure I'm accounting for everything properly. 3. Deal history and pipeline Can you send me a summary of every partnership or paid deal you've negotiated on my behalf since we started working together in April 2025? I want to have a complete record for my own accounting, and it would also help me understand what's worked well so we can do more of it. I'd also love an update on anything currently in the pipeline. 4. Inbound brand inquiries I've been forwarding brand inquiries that come to me directly over to the agency as agreed. Can you give me an update on what happened with those — which ones were pursued and the current status of each? 5. Merchandise I'm in the early stages of building my own merchandise line — things I design and sell independently, like apparel and prints. I want to make sure I understand how that fits within our agreement. Is self-created merchandise something I can pursue on my own, or is there anything in our contract I need to be aware of before I move forward? 6. Annual fee The contract mentions an annual agency fee of $250. Can you let me know the status of that — has it been charged, and if not, when should I expect it? 7. Payment timeline When a brand deal payment comes in, what's the typical turnaround before I receive my portion? I want to make sure I'm planning my cash flow accurately. Thank you again for everything. I'm looking forward to a great year ahead and excited about what we can build together. Alohi
